# Reference Manual Notes

This page is a condensed baseline from the Flightory Stallion V2 manual.

## Airframe baseline

- Configuration: twin tractor, single boom, V-tail
- Wingspan: 1340 mm
- Length: 990 mm
- Wing area: 26.5 dm2
- Airfoil: Eppler E205
- Cruise target: 60-70 km/h
- Recommended CG: 60 mm from wing-root leading edge
- Published all-up weight range: 1500-3000 g

## Recommended electronics (manual guidance)

- Motors: T-Motor F60 1750KV
- Props: 7x5, one CW and one CCW
- Flight controller: SpeedyBee F405 Wing
- GPS: Matek M10Q (Ended up not working)
- Servos: 4x EMAX ES08 MAII
- ESCs: 2x BLHeli_S 35A
- Battery: 4S 10,000 mAh Zeee
- Receiver: Matek R24-D

## Build-material intent from the manual

- LW-PLA for lightweight shell parts
- PETG for mounts, inserts, locks, and other load-bearing parts
- Low infill for shell sections, higher strength settings for structural parts

This split was one of the most important design rules in the whole project.

## Assembly details that affected this build

- Tailboom spec: 16 mm carbon tube, 430 mm cut length
- Servo wiring routes through the boom
- V-tail requires clean reinforcement and smooth, non-binding linkage movement
- Wing routing and lock fitment need careful dry fitting before final glue

## High-value preflight checks from the manual

- CG at 60 mm
- Correct control-surface direction
- Correct CW/CCW prop placement
- Correct opposite motor rotation
- Confident hand-launch technique

## Why keep this page

When troubleshooting started stacking up, this baseline helped separate manufacturer guidance from my own trial-and-error decisions.
